--- layout: default title: Ladies of Leisure ---

The Ladies of Leisure are women of independent means. They have no duties or responsibilities to anyone other than themselves. These gentlewomen navigate the modern world with tips, tricks and suggestions from the Good Housekeeping Guide of the 1960s. There’s nothing that can’t be solved by self-reliance, friendship and a strong martini.

Erin Marchak, Joan Patch, Candace Meeks and Tracy Bradley are the Ladies, and they have been improvising collectively for more than a decade. They’re alumni of Second City, Impatient Theatre, Bad Dog Theatre and the Toronto Fringe.
